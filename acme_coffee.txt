What are ACME Coffee Shop's opening hours?

ACME Coffee Shop is open from 7:00 AM to 5:00 PM, Monday through Friday. On weekends, we're open from 8:00 AM to 4:00 PM.

Yes, we offer a loyalty program where customers earn points for every purchase. These points can be redeemed for free drinks, discounts, and exclusive offers.

Currently we only take orders by phone.

Yes, we have a range of vegan and gluten-free options including pastries, sandwiches, and alternative milk choices for our coffee beverages.
